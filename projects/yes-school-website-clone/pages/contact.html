Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. On Saturdays, we operate from 8:00 AM to 12:30 PM for extracurricular activities and special programs. The administrative office is open from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM on weekdays.

How can I apply for admission?

To apply for admission, you can download the application form from our website or collect it from our admissions office. Complete the form with all required documents and submit it along with the application fee. After submission, eligible candidates will be called for an entrance test and interview.

Do you provide transportation services?

Yes, we provide transportation services to various areas within the city. Our buses are equipped with GPS tracking and safety features to ensure secure transportation. Transportation fees vary based on the distance from the school. Please contact our administrative office for specific route information and fees.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (basketball, cricket, swimming, tennis), arts (music, drama, dance, fine arts), clubs (science, robotics, debate, literature), and community service programs. These activities are designed to provide a well-rounded education and help students discover and develop their talents and interests.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

Campus tours are available by appointment on weekdays between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM. To schedule a tour, please contact our admissions office at +123 456 7891 or email admissions@yesschool.edu with your preferred date and time. We recommend scheduling tours at least a week in advance to ensure availability.
